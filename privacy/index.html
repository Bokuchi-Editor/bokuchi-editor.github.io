Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 9, 2026

This privacy policy explains how the Bokuchi website (bokuchi.com) handles information when you visit the site.

Analytics

This website uses Google Analytics 4 (GA4) to understand how visitors use the site. GA4 may collect the following information:

  • Pages visited and time spent on each page
  • Referring website or source
  • Browser type and operating system
  • Approximate geographic location (country/region level)
  • Device type (desktop, mobile, tablet)

This data is collected in aggregate and is not used to personally identify individual visitors. Google Analytics uses cookies to distinguish unique users. For more details, see Google's Privacy Policy.

About the Bokuchi Application

The Bokuchi desktop application does not collect any personal data, does not use analytics, and does not communicate with any external servers. All your documents remain on your local computer.

Cookies

This website uses cookies solely for the purpose of Google Analytics. No other cookies are used.

Third-Party Services

This website may link to external services such as GitHub. These services have their own privacy policies.

Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue on our GitHub repository.
