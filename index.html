Why Bokuchi?

🪶

Lightweight

Tauri + Rust keeps it far lighter than Electron-based editors. Uses less than a third of the memory.

⚡

Instant startup

Starts in under a second. Write whenever you're ready.

🌐

Works everywhere

macOS, Windows, and Linux. UI in 14 languages.

🔒

Private & offline

No account, no cloud, no tracking. Your documents stay on your computer.

Features

Real-time preview

Split view: edit on the left, live preview on the right. See your changes instantly as you type.

Math & diagrams

Render KaTeX math expressions and Mermaid diagrams directly in the preview. Perfect for technical and academic writing.

Markdown toolbar

Format text with toolbar buttons — no need to memorize Markdown syntax. Great for beginners and fast editing.

Folder tree & outline

Browse files in a sidebar folder tree and navigate your document with the heading outline panel.

Multi-tab editing

Open and manage multiple files at once. Your session is automatically restored when you relaunch.

Variable system

Use placeholders like {{author}} that expand when you export. Define global or per-file variables.

Cross-tab search

Search and replace across all open documents at once. Find what you need instantly.

Theme switcher

Choose from Default, Dark, Pastel, Vivid, and Darcula to match your style.
